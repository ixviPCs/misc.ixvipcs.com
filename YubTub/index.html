YubTub

A lightweight desktop app that opens YouTube in its own clean window.

⚠️ Important

  • This is not an official YouTube app.
  • It’s just a wrapper for convenience.
  • Do not redistribute YouTube content or claim it’s official.

Features

  • Opens YouTube in a frameless window.
  • Custom icon for easy identification.
  • Simple installation with desktop shortcut.

How to Use

  • Run the YubTub.Setup.1.4.0.exe installer.
  • Follow the steps in the installer.
  • Launch the app from your desktop or start menu.
  • Enjoy browsing YouTube like a standalone app!

Notes

  • This app requires Windows.
  • The app is self-contained—no extra DLLs needed.
